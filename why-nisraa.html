What is FiberHance™?

FiberHance™ is a patented hair repair technology developed through advanced cosmetic science. Unlike ordinary shampoos and conditioners that only coat the surface (cuticle) of the hair, FiberHance™ uses small molecules that penetrate deep into the hair cortex — the inner core where hair strength is determined.

Inside the cortex, FiberHance™ works to reinforce broken keratin bonds — the natural bonds that give hair its shape, elasticity, and strength. When these bonds are weakened by damage, heat, chemical treatment, or environmental stress, hair becomes brittle, prone to breakage, and difficult to manage.

FiberHance™ actively supports the rebuilding of these bonds, delivering results that go beyond cosmetic appearance — for hair that is genuinely stronger, more resilient, and healthier over time.
